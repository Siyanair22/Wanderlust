Dubai Itinerary: 5 Days of Glamour

Dubai blends modern luxury with cultural treasures. Discover skyscrapers, souks, and sand dunes in just 5 days!

Day 1: Downtown Dubai

  • Morning: Visit the Burj Khalifa.
  • Afternoon: Shop at Dubai Mall.
  • Evening: Watch the Dubai Fountain Show.
  • Food: Dine with a view of Burj Khalifa.

Day 2: Cultural Sights

  • Morning: Explore Dubai Museum and Al Fahidi District.
  • Afternoon: Ride an abra across Dubai Creek.
  • Evening: Wander through the Gold and Spice Souks.
  • Food: Try Emirati cuisine at a local restaurant.

Day 3: Palm & Beaches

  • Morning: Visit Palm Jumeirah and Atlantis.
  • Afternoon: Relax at JBR Beach.
  • Evening: Stroll at The Walk.
  • Food: Seafood dinner by the beach.

Day 4: Adventure

  • Morning: Explore Dubai Marina.
  • Afternoon: Skydive (optional) or boat cruise.
  • Evening: Enjoy a Desert Safari with BBQ dinner.
  • Food: Try dates and Arabic coffee.

Day 5: Modern Marvels

  • Morning: Visit Museum of the Future.
  • Afternoon: Explore City Walk and La Mer.
  • Evening: Farewell dinner at a rooftop restaurant.
  • Food: Indulge in international cuisines.

Bonus (Optional Day 6-7 ideas): Day trips to Abu Dhabi or Hatta Mountains.

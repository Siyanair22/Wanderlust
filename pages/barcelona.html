Barcelona Itinerary: 5 Days in the Catalan Capital

Barcelona is the perfect blend of architecture, beach vibes, and culture. Here's your ultimate 5-day guide!

Day 1: Gaudí’s Masterpieces

  • Morning: Visit the Sagrada Familia.
  • Afternoon: Explore Park Güell and Casa Batlló.
  • Evening: Stroll down La Rambla.
  • Food: Try churros and paella.

Day 2: Gothic Quarter

  • Morning: Visit the Barcelona Cathedral and Plaça del Rei.
  • Afternoon: Wander through El Born and Picasso Museum.
  • Evening: Dinner at a tapas bar in the Gothic Quarter.
  • Food: Try seafood paella and tapas.

Day 3: Beach & Montjuïc

  • Morning: Relax at Barceloneta Beach.
  • Afternoon: Explore Montjuïc and visit the Magic Fountain.
  • Evening: Enjoy panoramic views from Montjuïc Castle.
  • Food: Beachside seafood.

Day 4: Art & Culture

  • Morning: Visit the National Art Museum of Catalonia.
  • Afternoon: Explore Fundació Joan Miró.
  • Evening: Visit the Magic Fountain of Montjuïc.
  • Food: Catalan specialties.

Day 5: Shopping & Local Markets

  • Morning: Visit La Boqueria Market and shop at Passeig de Gràcia.
  • Afternoon: Explore El Raval neighborhood.
  • Evening: Farewell dinner at a rooftop bar with a view of the city.
  • Food: Try Catalan cream and turron.

Bonus (Optional Day 6-7 ideas): Day trip to Montserrat or Costa Brava.

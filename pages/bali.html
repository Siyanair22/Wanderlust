Bali Itinerary: 5 Days of Relaxation & Adventure

From lush jungles to pristine beaches, Bali is the ultimate escape for both relaxation and adventure. Here’s your perfect 5-day Bali itinerary!

Day 1: Ubud – Heart of Bali

  • Morning: Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary.
  • Afternoon: Explore the Tegallalang Rice Terraces.
  • Evening: Relax with a traditional Balinese massage.

Day 2: Beach Day

  • Morning: Enjoy water sports at Seminyak Beach.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Uluwatu Temple and watch the Kecak Fire Dance.
  • Evening: Dinner at a beachfront restaurant in Jimbaran Bay.

Day 3: Nusa Islands

  • Morning: Take a boat ride to Nusa Penida.
  • Afternoon: Explore Keling King Beach and Angel's Billabong.
  • Evening: Return to Bali and relax.

Day 4: Temples & Culture

  • Morning: Visit Tanah Lot Temple.
  • Afternoon: Explore Besakih Temple, Bali’s Mother Temple.
  • Evening: Enjoy a cultural show in Ubud.

Day 5: Nature & Adventure

  • Morning: Hike Mount Batur for sunrise.
  • Afternoon: Relax at Tirta Empul Temple.
  • Evening: Return to your hotel and unwind.

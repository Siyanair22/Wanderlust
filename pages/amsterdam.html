Amsterdam Itinerary: 5 Days in the Venice of the North

Amsterdam is known for its picturesque canals, rich history, and vibrant culture. Here's your ultimate 5-day guide!

Day 1: Historic Amsterdam

  • Morning: Visit the Anne Frank House.
  • Afternoon: Explore the Rijksmuseum and Van Gogh Museum.
  • Evening: Take a sunset Canal Cruise.
  • Food: Try Dutch pancakes and bitterballen.

Day 2: Culture & Parks

  • Morning: Stroll through Vondelpark.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Stedelijk Museum and explore Jordaan District.
  • Evening: Relax at a café in Leidseplein.
  • Food: Try poffertjes and fresh herring.

Day 3: Day Trips

  • Morning: Take a day trip to Keukenhof Gardens (if visiting in spring).
  • Afternoon: Visit Zaanse Schans or Volendam for traditional Dutch culture.
  • Evening: Return to Amsterdam and explore the Red Light District.
  • Food: Indulge in a Dutch cheese tasting.

Day 4: Amsterdam’s Neighborhoods

  • Morning: Visit the Bloemenmarkt (Flower Market).
  • Afternoon: Explore De Pijp and Albert Cuyp Market.
  • Evening: Experience the nightlife in Rembrandtplein.
  • Food: Try Indonesian rijsttafel (rice table).

Day 5: Offbeat Amsterdam

  • Morning: Explore the Hortus Botanicus (Botanical Gardens).
  • Afternoon: Visit the Amsterdam Museum and Hermitage Museum.
  • Evening: Enjoy a peaceful evening at Amsterdamse Bos (forest park).
  • Food: Savor Dutch fries with mayo and croquettes.

Bonus (Optional Day 6-7 ideas): Visit Gouda for cheese or explore the Haarlem region.

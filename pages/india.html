India Itinerary: 7 Days of Rich Culture

India, a land of vibrant colors and rich heritage, offers a truly unique experience. Here’s your ultimate 7-day guide to exploring temples, palaces, and bustling markets.

Day 1: Delhi – The Capital

  • Morning: Visit Red Fort and Qutub Minar.
  • Afternoon: Explore Humayun's Tomb and the India Gate.
  • Evening: Shop at Chandni Chowk and enjoy street food.

Day 2: Agra – The Taj Mahal

  • Morning: Visit the iconic Taj Mahal.
  • Afternoon: Explore Agra Fort and the Fatehpur Sikri.
  • Evening: Relax at the Mehtab Bagh for sunset views of the Taj.

Day 3: Jaipur – The Pink City

  • Morning: Visit Amber Fort and Jal Mahal.
  • Afternoon: Explore the City Palace and Hawa Mahal.
  • Evening: Shop for local crafts and enjoy Rajasthani cuisine.

Day 4: Varanasi – Spiritual Journey

  • Morning: Visit the Ghats of Varanasi for the early morning ritual.
  • Afternoon: Explore Kashi Vishwanath Temple.
  • Evening: Experience the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti.

Day 5: Mumbai – The City of Dreams

  • Morning: Visit the Gateway of India and Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus.
  • Afternoon: Explore Marine Drive and Colaba Causeway.
  • Evening: Enjoy a night out in the vibrant neighborhoods of Mumbai.

Day 6: Kerala – The Backwaters

  • Morning: Cruise through the Alleppey Backwaters in a houseboat.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • Evening: Relax on the beaches of Kovalam.

Day 7: Rajasthan – Forts and Palaces

  • Morning: Visit Mehrangarh Fort in Jodhpur.
  • Afternoon: Explore the Udaipur City Palace.
  • Evening: Watch a traditional Rajasthani performance.

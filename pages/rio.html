Rio de Janeiro Itinerary: 5 Days in Brazil’s Marvelous City

Rio is a city of samba, sun, and stunning landscapes. Here's your ultimate 5-day guide!

Day 1: Iconic Rio

  • Morning: Visit the Christ the Redeemer statue on Corcovado.
  • Afternoon: Explore Sugarloaf Mountain via cable car.
  • Evening: Relax at Copacabana Beach.
  • Food: Try feijoada (Brazilian stew) and caipirinha.

Day 2: Rio’s Beaches

  • Morning: Relax at Ipanema Beach.
  • Afternoon: Explore Lapa Arches and Selaron Steps.
  • Evening: Enjoy Rio's vibrant nightlife in Lapa.
  • Food: Try pão de queijo and grilled seafood.

Day 3: Forests & Gardens

  • Morning: Hike through Tijuca Forest.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Botanical Garden and the National Museum of Brazil.
  • Evening: Dine at a rooftop restaurant in Santa Teresa.
  • Food: Enjoy Brazilian BBQ at a churrascaria.

Day 4: Culture & History

  • Morning: Explore the Museum of Tomorrow and MAR (Museum of Art of Rio).
  • Afternoon: Walk around the Santa Teresa neighborhood.
  • Evening: Watch a samba performance at Rio Scenarium.
  • Food: Taste Brazilian empanadas and moqueca (seafood stew).

Day 5: Day Trips & Exploration

  • Morning: Take a day trip to Ilha Grande or Petropolis.
  • Afternoon: Explore Paraty (colonial town).
  • Evening: Return to Rio and enjoy a traditional dinner.
  • Food: Savor brigadeiros (Brazilian chocolate truffles).

Bonus (Optional Day 6-7 ideas): Visit the Maracanã Stadium or explore Angra dos Reis.

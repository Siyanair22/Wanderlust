Switzerland Itinerary: 7 Days of Alpine Adventure

Explore the majestic Alps, charming villages, and pristine lakes of Switzerland with this 7-day itinerary.

Day 1: Zurich

  • Morning: Visit Lake Zurich.
  • Afternoon: Explore Old Town (Altstadt).
  • Evening: Stroll along Bahnhofstrasse and enjoy Swiss chocolate.

Day 2: Lucerne

  • Morning: Take a boat tour of Lake Lucerne.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Chapel Bridge and the Swiss Transport Museum.
  • Evening: Enjoy the sunset from Mt. Pilatus.

Day 3: Interlaken

  • Morning: Take the train to Jungfraujoch, the Top of Europe.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Trummelbach Falls.
  • Evening: Relax in Interlaken’s town center.

Day 4: Zermatt

  • Morning: Take the Gornergrat Railway for views of the Matterhorn.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Glacier Palace.
  • Evening: Enjoy Swiss fondue in a local restaurant.

Day 5: Geneva

  • Morning: Explore Lake Geneva and the Jet d'Eau.
  • Afternoon: Visit the United Nations Office.
  • Evening: Relax in Parc des Bastions.

Day 6: Montreux

  • Morning: Visit the Chillon Castle.
  • Afternoon: Explore the Lavaux Vineyards.
  • Evening: Take a boat ride on Lake Geneva.

Day 7: Lausanne

  • Morning: Visit the Olympic Museum.
  • Afternoon: Stroll through Lausanne’s Old Town.
  • Evening: Enjoy dinner overlooking the lake.

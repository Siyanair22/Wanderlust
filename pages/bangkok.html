Bangkok Itinerary: 5 Days in Thailandâ€™s Heart

Bangkok offers a vibrant mix of temples, street food, and bustling markets. Here's your ultimate 5-day guide!

Day 1: Royal Bangkok

  • Morning: Visit the Grand Palace and Wat Phra Kaew.
  • Afternoon: Explore Wat Pho and the Reclining Buddha.
  • Evening: Take a sunset cruise on the Chao Phraya River.
  • Food: Try Pad Thai and Thai iced tea.

Day 2: Temples & Markets

  • Morning: Visit Wat Arun and explore its temples.
  • Afternoon: Wander through the Chatuchak Weekend Market.
  • Evening: Stroll through Khao San Road.
  • Food: Street food like satay and mango sticky rice.

Day 3: Ayutthaya Day Trip

  • Morning: Take a day trip to Ayutthaya (historical ruins).
  • Afternoon: Explore Wat Chaiwatthanaram and Ayutthaya Historical Park.
  • Evening: Return to Bangkok.
  • Food: Enjoy a riverside lunch in Ayutthaya.

Day 4: Shopping & Relaxation

  • Morning: Shop at MBK Center or Terminal 21.
  • Afternoon: Visit Siam Paragon and Jim Thompson House.
  • Evening: Relax at a spa or enjoy a rooftop cocktail.
  • Food: Thai curry and spicy noodles.

Day 5: Cultural Exploration

  • Morning: Explore the Bangkok National Museum.
  • Afternoon: Visit Chinatown and Yaowarat Road.
  • Evening: Dinner at a riverside restaurant with a view of the Wat Arun lit up at night.
  • Food: Dim sum or Thai-style barbecue.

Bonus (Optional Day 6-7 ideas): Day trip to Damnoen Saduak Floating Market or Safari World.

Paris Itinerary: 5 Days of Magic

Paris isn’t just a city; it’s a feeling. Here’s a day-by-day plan to help you experience the best of Paris – from famous landmarks to hidden gems, mouthwatering food, and cozy cafes.

Day 1: Iconic Landmarks

  • Morning: Visit the Eiffel Tower. Book tickets online to skip the line!
  • Afternoon: Picnic at Champ de Mars and stroll along the Seine River.
  • Evening: Seine River Cruise for sunset views.
  • Food: Try Café de Flore for classic French brunch.

Day 2: Art & History

  • Morning: Explore the Louvre Museum (don’t miss the Mona Lisa).
  • Afternoon: Visit the Notre-Dame Cathedral and nearby Latin Quarter.
  • Evening: Relax at Jardin du Luxembourg.
  • Food: Lunch at Le Procope — Paris’ oldest café!

Day 3: Royal Grandeur

  • Morning: Day trip to the Palace of Versailles (gardens and Hall of Mirrors).
  • Afternoon: Explore the Versailles town markets.
  • Evening: Return to Paris and relax.
  • Food: Taste pastries at Angelina Paris (famous for hot chocolate).

Day 4: Artistic Paris

  • Morning: Wander around Montmartre and visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  • Afternoon: Explore artists' square, Place du Tertre.
  • Evening: Attend a cabaret show at Moulin Rouge (optional).
  • Food: Enjoy dinner at Le Consulat, a historic Montmartre restaurant.

Day 5: Shopping & Farewell

  • Morning: Shop along the Champs-Élysées and visit the Arc de Triomphe.
  • Afternoon: Explore the boutiques of Le Marais or relax in Place des Vosges.
  • Evening: Final Parisian dinner with a view at Terrass'' Hotel Rooftop.
  • Food: Try classic French dishes like duck confit or coq au vin.

Bonus (Optional Day 6-7 ideas): Visit Disneyland Paris, day trip to Giverny (Monet’s Gardens), or explore Père Lachaise Cemetery and Canal Saint-Martin for a more local vibe.

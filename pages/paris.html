Welcome to Paris

Paris, the capital city of France, is famed for its romantic ambiance, stunning architecture, world-class museums, and vibrant culture. From the iconic Eiffel Tower to charming cafes, every corner of Paris tells a story.

Top Places to Visit:

  • Eiffel Tower The symbol of Paris and a must-visit.
  • Louvre Museum Home to the Mona Lisa and thousands of art pieces.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral Gothic architecture at its finest.
  • Montmartre An artsy neighborhood known for the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
  • Seine River Cruise Explore Paris by boat.
  • Palace of Versailles A lavish royal residence just outside Paris.
  • Champs-Élysées & Arc de Triomphe Stroll through Paris' most famous avenue.

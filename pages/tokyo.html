Tokyo Itinerary: 5 Days of Adventure

Tokyo is a dazzling mix of ancient traditions and futuristic wonders. Here's your ultimate 5-day guide to explore shrines, neon streets, delicious food, and pop culture!

Day 1: Traditional Tokyo

  • Morning: Visit Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa and explore Nakamise Shopping Street.
  • Afternoon: Walk through Ueno Park and visit museums or the zoo.
  • Evening: Dine in Ameya-Yokocho Market.
  • Food: Try street snacks like melonpan and yakitori.

Day 2: Modern Marvels

  • Morning: Explore Shibuya — experience the famous Scramble Crossing and Hachiko statue.
  • Afternoon: Shop at Shinjuku (Don Quijote, department stores).
  • Evening: View Tokyo from Tokyo Metropolitan Government Building (free observatory).
  • Food: Slurp ramen at Ichiran or Afuri.

Day 3: Pop Culture & Tech

  • Morning: Dive into Akihabara — anime, manga, and electronics paradise.
  • Afternoon: Visit the futuristic teamLab Planets or Borderless Museum.
  • Evening: Chill at Odaiba seaside park and see the Rainbow Bridge lights.
  • Food: Try a themed café (Maid Café, Pokémon Café).

Day 4: Nature and Relaxation

  • Morning: Walk through Meiji Shrine and Yoyogi Park.
  • Afternoon: Explore trendy Harajuku and Omotesando for shopping and crepes.
  • Evening: Relax at an onsen like Oedo-Onsen Monogatari (hot springs).
  • Food: Grab colorful desserts at Takeshita Street.

Day 5: Cultural Farewell

  • Morning: Visit the Imperial Palace East Gardens.
  • Afternoon: Explore Ginza for high-end shopping or visit Tsukiji Outer Market for fresh sushi.
  • Evening: Last night stroll at Tokyo Tower — sparkling views.
  • Food: Splurge on omakase sushi or savor tempura at a local izakaya.

Bonus (Optional Day 6-7 ideas): Day trips to Mt. Fuji (Hakone) or Nikko, or explore hidden gems like Shimokitazawa and Yanaka Ginza.

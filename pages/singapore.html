Singapore Itinerary: 5 Days in the Lion City

Singapore is a city of innovation, nature, and culture. Here's your ultimate 5-day guide to this modern paradise!

Day 1: Modern Singapore

  • Morning: Visit the Marina Bay Sands SkyPark.
  • Afternoon: Explore Gardens by the Bay and the Flower Dome.
  • Evening: Watch the Supertree Grove Light Show.
  • Food: Try chili crab and kaya toast.

Day 2: Culture & Heritage

  • Morning: Visit the Singapore National Museum.
  • Afternoon: Explore Chinatown and the Chinatown Heritage Centre.
  • Evening: Visit Little India and explore its temples.
  • Food: Indian curry and roti prata.

Day 3: Sentosa Island

  • Morning: Visit Universal Studios Singapore.
  • Afternoon: Relax at Sentosa Beach or explore SEA Aquarium.
  • Evening: Enjoy the Wings of Time light show.
  • Food: Seafood at the Sentosa boardwalk.

Day 4: Nature & Wildlife

  • Morning: Visit Singapore Zoo or the River Safari.
  • Afternoon: Explore the Jurong Bird Park.
  • Evening: Dinner at Clarke Quay.
  • Food: Hainanese chicken rice and laksa.

Day 5: Shopping & Relaxation

  • Morning: Shop at Orchard Road.
  • Afternoon: Explore the Singapore Flyer.
  • Evening: Sunset view from East Coast Park.
  • Food: Try dim sum or satay.

Bonus (Optional Day 6-7 ideas): Visit Pulau Ubin or explore the Botanic Gardens.
